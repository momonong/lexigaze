This engine replaces dense neural attention with causal physical momentum and cognitive skipping/regression penalties.

## 2. Methodology Update

### A. POM Transition Logic (Skill 10)
The transition matrix $T$ is constructed using three biological principles:
1.  **Forward Momentum**: A Gaussian distribution $P_{fwd}$ centered at the next word ($i+1$), modeling the default reading saccade.
2.  **Skipping Penalty**: The forward probability to word $j$ is penalized by its Cognitive Mass ($CM_j$). Difficult words (high CM) are less likely to be skipped.
3.  **Regression Boost**: Backward probabilities ($j \le i$) are modeled with an exponential decay from the preceding word ($i-1$) and boosted by the difficulty of the *current* word ($CM_i$).

### B. Mathematical Refinement
Initial implementation resulted in an accuracy of **1.28%** due to non-normalized Cognitive Mass values causing negative probabilities and zero-transition deadlocks in the Viterbi decoder. 
The model was refined with:
- **Min-Max Normalization**: Ensuring CM values are strictly within $[0, 1]$.
- **Regression Baseline**: Adding a $+0.1$ epsilon to regression scores to ensure Viterbi can maintain "Stay" states during multi-frame fixations.

## 4. Discussion

### Why POM is the NeurIPS Choice
While LLM attention (STOCK-T v1/v2) is intellectually stimulating, it lacks the causal "forward-only" nature of real-time human reading. POM provides a deterministic, biologically grounded framework that is:
1.  **Interpretable**: Clear parameters for forward spread and regression sensitivity.
2.  **Efficient**: Low-latency calculation without needing to load full attention tensors.
3.  **Causal**: Respects the temporal flow of information processing.
